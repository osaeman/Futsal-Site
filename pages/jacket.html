Work Jacket

NFPA2112
NFPA 70 E HRC2
  • The garment is double stitched with high resistance flame retardant threads.
  • In the lower part, two leaned pockets with lining of flame retardant fabric.
  • The jacket reaches 10 to 12 centimeters (cm), below the waist.
  • Front fastening system with flame retardant zipper.
  • Waistband at the bottom and two loops at the waist adjusted by velcro.
  • Back patched pockets, these pockets have a reinforcement of the same fabric in its lower part
  • Reinforcements at the end of the pockets
  • The waistband has double fabric
  • The adjustment system is by means of a four-hole flame-resistant material button of flat, smooth type, they are sewn with reinforcements and final details. The buttonhole is straight with closed stitches properly finished
  • The waistband has six pins (loops) distributed equidistant from the same fabric sewn to the trouser
  • Double stitching throughout the garment
  • Overlock seams and security stitches inside the garment
  • 100% sewing thread flame retardant fibers (ARAMIDAS) is used to withstand high temperatures on contact with fire, heat and electric arc.
  • In the front, a zipper reinforced with flame retardant materials. It is sewn with double straight stitching and finished on the bottom. The closure has a lock that does not allow its sliding when in use.
  • In each of the coarse, at 15 cm below the knee has a 1" wide reflective gray tape.
